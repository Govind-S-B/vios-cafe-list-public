# Starbucks Vellayambalam

My go to place for work. Large open space vibe and its 24x7 open. Love the baristas, gets chill once you are a regular there. Folks either come there for a hangout or to work/study.

Hot choco is good but a bit expensive so I always make the stay worth it.
